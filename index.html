About COCAS

College of Commerce, Arts and Science is situated in Patna in Bihar state of India. Established in 1949, it is accredited from UGC, NAAC, AICTE and it is affiliated to Magadh University. COCAS, Patna offers 34 courses across 11 streams namely Science, Commerce & Banking, Arts, Management, IT, Vocational, Medical, Paramedical, Agriculture, Media & Mass Communication, Law and across 16 degrees like BSc, B.Com, BA, BBM, BCA.Hostel facility is not available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Academic Zone, Library, Medical Facilities, Cls. Room, Placement, Sports, Residential Institute, Residential Zone, Residential Faculty Zone are also there.
